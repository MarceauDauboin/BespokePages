Services

What We Do.

Every Business and Individual deserves to share their ideas to the world. At Bespoke Pages we believe the best way to achieve this in our modern environment is trough an immaculate & custom made proffesional website, one that is tailor made for your needs & your brand's unique vision.

Web Design

Your website will be designed to the highest standards, allowing for an elegant yet simple to use User Interface. You are always welcome to add a personal touch or modification during the design process but we will put our utmost effort into creating a site that matches both your brand's identity & style.

From R1500 | $110

Web Development

Once we have designed your website(or received an existing design) we will begin to develop & code it. Using HTML5 as well as CSS3 we are able to provide a strong web foundation from which its contents can be built upon. We ensure that our websites are responsive(work on all devices) & use encrypted HTTPS protocols to ensure the safety of your users' connections.

From R1500 | $110

Hosting

All of our produced websites are hosted for FREE on secure Github servers with no external advertisements.
Your free domain will resemble this template: yourName.github.io/yourCompanyName/
A more traditional domain can be registered via a third party & managed by Bespoke Pages but these incur external costs.

Included

Search Engine Optimization
(SEO)

We provide both Advanced and Basic SEO services. All of our websites include standard web optimization to ensure they are organically found through any search engine with minimal bloat. Our advanced SEO services incur an extra fee for superior search positions & site statistics, using extended keyword libraries & thorough page optimisation, increasing page response times.

Advanced: From R1000 | $70
Basic: Included

Frequently asked questions
Project Time to Completion

We aim to keep our websites at their highest possible standards which is why we require 1-2 weeks to complete each project. If the site is required in a shorter amount of time we shall discuss an "express" fee dependent on your needs.

Payment & Refunds

At least 50% of the payment must be made once you are satisfied with the overall direction of the site as we will be continuously consulting you on your website's needs.
Full payment is required once the website is completed with ownership of the site being transfered over to you.

The 50% deposit is only due once you are satisified with the overall direction of the site & is therefore non-refundable.
If you are not 100% satisfied with the final product, we shall continue to itterate until it meets your needs.
Yet if you are still not satisified, final payment is NOT required. We shall however retain the site's ownership.

Translation

All of our websites are written in english but can be translated to French - From R500(or $35) Other languages are possible but availability & price are dependant on a case by case basis.

Ownership

Once you are satisified with your site & have made the final payment we shall transfer ownership of the site to you, which includes the domain & source code.
